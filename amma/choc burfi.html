Chocolate Burfi

Ingredients

  • 1 tub ricotta
  • butter
  • sugar
  • carnation milk
  • chocolate
  • vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Remove all moisture from ricotta by placing in cloth for 1 day
  2. Melt 1 and ½ stick of butter on stove
  3. Add ricotta to butter and stir until soft and shiny (some of the ghee gets extracted)
  4. Add about 2 cups of sugar and mix
  5. Add about 1 and ½ cups of carnation milk gradually until the proper consistency is achieved (it should be like a very loose dough)
  6. Add about 1 and ½ teaspoons of vanilla extract
  7. Lay out in a pan lined with cling wrap
  8. Melt ½ stick of butter in a pot
  9. Add 2 cups of semi sweet chocolate chips and melt
  10. Pour on top of the milk burfi and overnight
