Amma's Recipes

Amma's Recipes are a list of recipes collected by me (and a few others) in attempt to preserve our mom's cooking. Since we aren't the greatest cooks we need all the help we can get to make sure we can still eat this amazing food.

Mains

Sambar

Sambar is a hearty soup that can be the base of a meal. It usually consists of vegetables and lentils and is commonly eaten with dosa or rice (or perhaps quinoa if you want to be healthier).
