The Year in Photos - 2015

The World Series

October 2015

The Mets made it to the world series this year and I was there! Unfortunately, for me (and the Mets), it didn't end well. But the core of the team is strong and next year should be a good one.

Italy

August 2015

Earlier this year we took a family trip to Italy and spent a few days in Rome and a few more days in Tuscany.

Zion Narrows

May 2015

Roughly around memorial day, we celebrated by wife's friends birthday by doing a small but absolutely beautiful hike The Narrows in Zion National Park. The Narrows is the narrowest section of Zion Canyon. It was amazing.

Skiing in Vail

January 2015

My wife and I kicked off the year with a trip to Vail. Both of us enjoy skiing but rarely get a chance to go. We creatively used our airline miles to get ridiculously good deal to Vail and did a couple of days of skiing with a couple of other people. That was a lot of fun.
